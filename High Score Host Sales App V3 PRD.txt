High Score Host Sales App (V3)
Philosophy
The app should do as little as possible.

Everything that changes frequently should live outside the app.

The app's primary job is to authenticate salespeople, connect them to Stripe, and provide a simple launcher for sales resources.

Core Features
1. Login
Salesperson creates an account.

Admin activates account.

Inactive accounts cannot access Stripe links.

2. Dashboard
Very simple.

Buttons only.

Examples:

Sales Deck

Pricing

Product Info

Videos

Launch Kits

Checkout

Announcements

Training

Help

Everything opens a link.

3. Checkout
The most important feature.

Displays:

Launch Kits

Pricing

Discounts

Stripe Payment Links

Copy Link

Share Link

QR Code

Every sale ultimately goes through Stripe.

4. QR Code
Display QR codes for:

Demo

Customer Purchase

Personal Referral

Nothing more complicated.

5. Admin
Admin can edit:

Button names

Button order

Destination URLs

Stripe links

Announcements

Account activation

Everything updates instantly.
